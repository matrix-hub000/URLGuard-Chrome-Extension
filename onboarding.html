Getting Started

1
Click the URLGuard icon

Find the URLGuard shield icon in your browser toolbar. Click it to see the status of the current website.

2
Configure API keys (optional)

For enhanced AI-powered analysis, add your DeepSeek and OpenAI API keys in the Settings tab. The extension works with basic protection even without API keys.

3
Configure your preferences

Adjust your notification settings, history tracking options in the Settings tab.

4
Manage trusted domains

Add trusted websites to your whitelist or known malicious sites to your blacklist.

5
Browse with confidence

URLGuard will monitor your browsing and alert you when it detects potential threats.
